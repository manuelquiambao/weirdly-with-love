“We get inspiration from the stories people tell.

What about stories we barely get to listen to at all?”

"weirdly, with love" is an Interactive Media website project by Manuel Quiambao focusing on the culture, folklore, and current happenings of marginalized communities and minorities across Canada. The website aims to depict these stories in an empathic, decolonial, and collaborative light. As such, its content will be focused on discourses between people within these cultures.

"weirdly, with love" was initially seen as a method to explore and highlight folklore from marginalized cultures due to fantasy tropes being built on mostly Western perspectives and approaches on fantasy. As development grew, "weirdly, with love" transformed into a more generalized website aiming to share the lives of people from these cultures with a more empathic mindset -- aiming to "give the mic" to others. Even so, the idea of highlighting folklore for inspiring creative pursuits still persists in the vision for the website.

About the Creator

Manuel Quiambao (he/him) is a Filipino-born Canadian studying as an undergraduate in OCAD University studying under Digital Futures. He is interdisciplinary in his creative pursuits mainly focusing on character design, worldbuilding, lore-writing, and game design. He mainly explores fantasy with whimsy and wonder inspired by video games and other cultural media while incorporating personal themes such as identity, disability, and race.
